ἀγάπη

agapē ah-gah-pay
Noun Nominative Fem. Singular Strong's G26

Dictionary Definition

1. Love; specifically brotherly love, affection, good will.
2. Benevolence; the high form of love which sees something infinitely precious in its object.

Contextual Insight

AI

In 1 Corinthians 13, Paul elevates agapé above all other spiritual gifts. Unlike eros (romantic) or phileo (friendship), this word denotes a willful, sacrificial love that is not based on the merit of the beloved but on the character of the lover.

Compare with John 3:16 where the same root is used for God's love toward the world.
